Consultancy Services Offered by Santosh Doshi

1. Import Export Consultancy

Description: This service focuses on guiding new exporters through the processes involved in import and export. It includes networking, document handling, registration guidance, quality control, and onboarding.

Scope of Work:

  • Networking and calling new exporters.
  • Training and guidance for new exporters.
  • Coordination with exporters regarding official documentation and liaison.
  • Quality control of goods during export (both physical and virtual).
  • Visits to export institutions for networking and promotion.

Remuneration: Rs 20,000 per month as a fixed fee, which is standard in the industry.

2. Third Party Inspection (TPI) Services

Description: Provides third-party inspection services to ensure the quality and standards of goods at the time of export.

Details:

  • Inspection of goods to meet quality standards.
  • Coordination with clients and vendors for smooth operations.
  • Regular updates and reports to clients.

3. BPO and BPM Services

Description: Consulting services for setting up and managing BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) and BPM (Business Process Management) operations. This includes process identification, setup, and ongoing management.

Commission Structure:

  • 15% consulting fee on the initial deposit paid by the client.
  • 7% commission on monthly billing before tax, covering regular coordination, monitoring, and process improvement.

4. Dubai Business Consulting

Description: Specialized consulting for businesses looking to expand or establish operations in Dubai. Services include market research, business setup guidance, logistics support, and compliance management.

Additional Services:

  • Assistance in company formation and licensing in Dubai.
  • Support with finding local sponsors and partners.
  • Guidance on Dubai's tax and regulatory environment.
  • Market entry strategy and competitor analysis.

5. Corporate Training and Support

Description: Offers corporate training programs in import-export processes, quality control, logistics management, and other related fields.

Key Offerings:

  • Training videos and live sessions.
  • Support for logistics and technical guidance.
  • Customized training programs for different corporate needs.
